Our mission

Setting up of a startup in Nepal, is such a hard thing to do. And sustaining a business is even much harder. We hardly have handful of companies making profit, which has converted the Nepali market into monopoly. In order to improve economic conditions of Nepal, we need to setup more startups and compete with the world.

Balance Group is on a mission to bootstrap a startup movement in Nepal. We provide next-generation platform to those innovative entrepreneurs who are ready to work hard and expand their business to new possibilities.

As a group of companies we are targeting to go public in next 10 years and becoming biggest tax payer company in Nepal. Our highest priority is to become a value based company, which make the lifes of not only employees but people of Nepal better.
