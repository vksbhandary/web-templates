Collaborate With US

Balance Group is a multipurpose company with an aim to invest in various business sectors in Nepal. We provide a platform to those innovative entrepreneurs who are ready to grow and work hard. We focus on all major sectors of Nepal, like Tourism, Agriculture, Information technlogy, Artificial Inteligence.

Based on entrepreneur's dedication and resources, we provide different ways to collaborate with us.

Human Resource investment

The concerned person should be willing to fully immerse himself and (or) his team (work fulltime) in order to build a startup. This type of collaboration can help those entrepreneurs, who are dedicated but don't have investment to start new company. They must possess mendatory knowledge or skill or must be able to hire employees, to solve the proposed problem. They should be able to lead effectively. They are not supposed to invest any money, rather they will be paid money for every drop of hardwork. Also they will own a pre settled percentage of company profit.

Financial investment

The concerned person should be willing to invest some amount of money on any of our sister company or project. They will get all the benifits of being our investor. Which includes ...

Human Resource and Financial investment

The concerned person should be willing to invest some amount of money on their own company and work full time as a Human Resource investor. This type of collaboration method is supposed to help those entrepreneurs who have some money but not enough to start their business on their own. We provide funding to these individuals and they get benifits of being both HR investor and Financial investor. That means they will also get monthly salary and share of profit as a investor.
