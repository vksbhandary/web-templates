ABOUT US

Balance Group is a multipurpose company with an aim to invest in various business sectors in Nepal. We provide a platform to those innovative entrepreneurs who are ready to grow and work hard. We focus on all major sectors of Nepal, like Tourism, Agriculture, Information technlogy, Artificial Inteligence.

We aim to provide long term capital growth to all our investors in various sectors. We actively seek new partners, with similar vision, for investment opportunities.

Balance Group is focused on nurturing the businesses as a family, by sharing our expertise, valuable assets, insights, market research and market evaluation, providing guidance to young entrepreneurs.
